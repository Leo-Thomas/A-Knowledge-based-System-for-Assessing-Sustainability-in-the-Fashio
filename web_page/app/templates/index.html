Instructions

Using the numeric scale, enter the answer that you consider the most appropriate description for your company according to each indicator. Higher values mean that your company has better measures to increase sustainability for that indicator. Lower values mean poorer measures to enhance sustainability for that indicator.


For example, if your company has excellent measures to reduce carbon emissions, you should rate that indicator as "high." Similarly, if your company has excellent measures to increase the amount of recycled material used, you should also rate that indicator as "high." On the other hand, if your company has poor measures in controlling water usage, which means excessive use of this resource, you should rate this indicator as "poor."
